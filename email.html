Hello <First name>,

At The AA, nothing matters more to us than motorists. So we’ve spoken to a panel of members like you about what’s important to them. And we’ve brought that insight together in a manifesto for government, journalists and civil servants.

We’re calling on all political parties to listen to the needs of drivers, and take action on our recommendations to help shape a better road ahead for all motorists.

Our manifesto highlights

 
Supporting drivers
Smooth journeys
Safe roads
 
SUPPORTING DRIVERS

Costs and affordability

For many people, driving isn’t a luxury – it’s a vital means of mobility. We believe that retaining a freeze on fuel duty, increasing fuel price transparency, and reducing insurance premium tax can help to keep costs low.

SMOOTHER JOURNEY

Traffic management and infrastructure

From potholes and flooding to smart motorways, the state of the roads was by far the most important issue for our members. We recommend scrapping smart motorways and increasing long-term funding of local road maintenance for permanent repairs.

 
 
SAFER ROADS

Saving lives

On average, five people a day are killed on our roads and 82 are seriously injured. We’ve looked at 9 ways to reduce this – including reintroducing road safety targets, maintaining driver training and increasing road traffic officers – because road death needs to be higher on the political agenda.

Read the full manifesto for a comprehensive look at all the topics on our agenda – including driving a sustainable future and how we’re strengthening the industry.
